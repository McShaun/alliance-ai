# ALLIANCE Hard Mechanics

## Resource Tokens
- **Green**: Finance
- **Red**: Cheap Labor / Infrastructure
- **Blue**: Weapons Technology
- **Black**: Natural Resources
- **White**: Science / Civil Institutions

## Adjudication Scale (1-4)
When adjudicating an Action Report (AR), assign one of the following difficulty levels:
1. **Easy / Reasonable**: Automatic success.
2. **Likely to Succeed**: Draw from the full 6-card Fate Deck. Success unless 'FAIL' is drawn.
3. **Difficult**: Remove one 'RISKY' card from the Fate Deck before drawing (draw from 5 cards). The player must fulfill the specific conditions for success on the drawn Fate Card.
4. **Very Difficult**: Only a 'SUCCESS' or 'VERY SUCCESSFUL' ('GREAT SUCCESS') card counts as a win. All other results are failures.

## Battle Advantage & Destruction Rules
When resolving conflicts (like war or opposed actions):
- **Advantage**: To grant advantage to one side, remove the 2-pip dice card titled "Risky" from the Fate Deck before that player draws. 
- **Major Advantage / Disadvantage**: Restore the "Risky" card and remove a 5-pip "Success" card before the disadvantaged player draws from the deck.
- **Resolution**: The player who draws the higher card wins. 
- **Destruction Rule**: If the difference between the dice pips illustrated on the drawn cards is 3 or more, the losing side's standing army is destroyed.
