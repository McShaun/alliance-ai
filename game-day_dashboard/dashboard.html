Tokens Guide

Blue Token Weapons Tech can be gambled to build weapons or win battles
Green Token 2 Finance Credit can be traded for one of any other color token
Black Token Spend 1 Black and 1 Green to move a military outside your borders
Red Token Spend to build infrastructure or mass produce new science
White Token Gamble to Invent New Technology

First Turn Suggestions

1 Read Your National Agenda

Try to achieve your initial objectives to make progress on the Status track.

2 Prepare your first Action Report

Based on the Secret Objectives on the back of your National Agenda, or come up with a unique agenda.

3 Find out What Your Neighbors Want

Maybe you have a surplus of Tokens that they have a deficiency in. Or perhaps you can pool your resources to achieve shared objectives.

Form an ALLIANCE

It's up to you to formalize your agreements. It might help to get something in writing.

Call a U.N. meeting

Use this to settle disputes among multiple partners.

Please Submit your first Action Report

Each team should choose a pre-written Action Report or fill out a blank Action Report and submit it to the Facilitator by the end of the Game Day.
